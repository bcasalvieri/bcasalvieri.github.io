Hi, I'm Brad! I am a full stack web developer living in New Jersey.

My journey to being a developer was circuitous. I attended the University of Delaware, graduating in 3.5 years with a BS in Exercise Science while also completing a pre-med track. Upon graduation, I decided to take a break from formal education and worked in a mentoring ministry followed by 6 years as an educator and coach. Desiring a change that allowed me to better utilize my creativity, I enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp to become a Full Stack Web Developer, and quickly developed a passion for technology and creating beautiful and meaningful websites and applications.

In college, I was a member of the varsity football team, which developed my natural born leadership skills, instilled a passion for utilizing teamwork, creativity, and innovation to solve complex problems, and solidified my belief to serve others. Furthermore, as a quick learner, my college experience instilled a passion for continuous learning, which I carry into my newfound career in technology.
