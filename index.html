Hi, I'm Brad! I am a full stack web developer living in New Jersey.

My journey to become a developer was a circuitous one. I attended the University of Delaware, graduating in 3.5 years with a BS in Exercise Science while also completing a pre-med track. Upon graduation, I decided to take a break from formal education and worked in a mentoring ministry followed by 6 years as an educator and coach. Desiring a change that allowed me to better utilize my creativity, I enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp to become a Full Stack Web Developer, and quickly discovered a passion for technology and creating beautiful, meaningful websites and applications.

In college, I was a member of the varsity football team, which strengthened my natural born leadership skills, instilled a passion for utilizing teamwork and innovation to solve complex problems, and solidified my desire to serve others. Furthermore, as a quick learner, my college experience instilled a passion for continuous learning, which I carry into my newfound career in technology.
